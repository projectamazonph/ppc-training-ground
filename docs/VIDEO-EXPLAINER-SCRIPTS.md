# Video Explainer Script Pack. Foundations Launch

These are short, screen-led explainers for Filipino aspiring VAs. Record at a calm pace, use captions, and show the learner exactly where the decision happens. Do not use a talking head for more than the opening and close. The screen, spreadsheet, and simulator should carry the lesson.

## Production rules

- Runtime: 3 to 5 minutes each.
- Format: 16:9 primary video. Keep every essential label in the centre area for mobile viewing.
- Captions: burned in and downloadable transcript.
- Visual language: large numbers, one decision per screen, high contrast, no tiny console screenshots.
- Ending: one clear action in AMPH. No vague "keep learning" close.
- Source note: any Amazon Ads interface or product behaviour shown must be rechecked before recording.

## 3. Break-Even ACoS and Maximum CPC

**Course placement:** Module 1, after the Big Six overview.

**Learner outcome:** Calculate the two numbers that make bids defensible.

|---|---|---|

| 0:35â€“1:20 | Formula appears: break-even ACoS = profit before ads Ã· selling price. | "Break-even ACoS is the highest advertising cost you can carry before ad-attributed sales stop producing contribution profit. | 1:20â€“2:10 | Formula appears: max CPC = selling price Ã— target ACoS Ã— conversion rate. | "Next, calculate a maximum CPC. | 2:10â€“3:00 | Show a bid of $1.20 versus max CPC $0.75. | "If market clicks cost one dollar twenty, that does not mean you force the bid to one dollar twenty.
