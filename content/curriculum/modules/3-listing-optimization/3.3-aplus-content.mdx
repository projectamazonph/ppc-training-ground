## How A+ Content Boosts PPC Performance

### The Conversion Rate Lift

Amazon's own data shows that A+ Content improves conversion rates by **5-17%** on average. For PPC, this is huge:

| Scenario | Conversion Rate | ACoS at $0.75 CPC |
|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| No A+ Content | 6% | 50% |
| Basic A+ Content | 8% | 38% |
| Premium A+ Content | 10% | 30% |

**Same product. Same ad. Same bid. Better listing = 20 percentage points lower ACoS.**

## Brand Analytics for PPC

Once you have Brand Registry, you unlock **Brand Analytics** вҖ” a data source that transforms your keyword research:

### What Brand Analytics Tells You

| Report | What It Shows | How to Use for PPC |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Amazon Search Terms** | Actual search frequency for ANY keyword | Discover high-volume keywords competitors use |
| **Item Comparison** | Which products shoppers compare yours to | Find cross-sell and competitive keyword targets |
| **Alternate Purchase Behavior** | What shoppers buy instead of your product | Identify competitor ASINs for targeting |
| **Top Search Terms** | Your brand's top converting search terms | Double down on what works |
| **Demographics** | Age, income, education of your buyers | Adjust bidding and ad schedule |

**Real example:**
Without Brand Analytics, you're guessing that "organic cutting board" gets 5,000 monthly searches. With Brand Analytics, you know it gets **3,247** вҖ” and you know the top 3 products in that search, their prices, and their click share. That's not guessing. That's data.

> рҹ’Ў **Tip**: Use Brand Analytics search term data to find keywords with **high frequency and low competition** вҖ” those are your gold mines. They have volume but few advertisers bidding on them. You'll get cheap clicks and high conversion rates.
