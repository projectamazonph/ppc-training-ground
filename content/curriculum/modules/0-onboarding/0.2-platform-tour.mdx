# Platform Tour & Navigation

Welcome to your cockpit! Everything you need is one click away. Let's take a quick tour so you know exactly where to find things.

> 🎯 **Analogy**: Think of AdCraft like a car. The sidebar is your dashboard — it has all the buttons you need. The main area is the windshield — that's where the action happens. And the AI Mentor? That's your GPS, ready to reroute you anytime you get lost.

## The Sidebar — Your Dashboard

The sidebar on the left has five sections. Here's what each one does:

| Section | What It Does | When You'll Use It |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your home base — shows progress, XP, and quick links | Every time you open AdCraft |
| **Modules** | Structured lessons organized by topic | When you're learning new concepts |
| **Simulations** | Interactive practice scenarios | When you're ready to apply what you learned |
| **AI Mentor** | Chat-based PPC coach | When you're stuck or curious |
| **Team** | Your organization and team progress | When you're part of a team |

At the bottom of the sidebar, you'll see your **XP** and **level** — these grow as you complete lessons and simulations.

> 💡 **Tip**: Try clicking each section now to see what's inside. Don't worry about understanding everything yet — you're just getting familiar with the layout.

## Dashboard — The Big Picture

Your dashboard answers one question: **"What should I do next?"**

- **Welcome card** — Shows your current level and XP progress
- **Stats row** — How many modules you've finished, simulations completed, lessons done
- **Module cards** — Each module shows a progress bar and whether it's locked, in progress, or complete
- **Quick actions** — One-click buttons to continue learning, start a simulation, or ask the AI Mentor

## Modules — Your Learning Path

The Modules view shows all five learning modules in order:

| Module | Title | What You'll Learn | Number of Lessons |
|--------|-------|------------------|------------------|
| 0 | Onboarding | Platform tour, first simulation intro | 3 |
| 1 | Foundations | PPC metrics — CPC, ACoS, ROAS, and more | 5 |
| 4 | Campaign Architecture | Building proper campaign structures | 4 |
| 6 | Bidding Lab | When and how much to bid | 3 |
| 7 | Search Term Triage | Finding and fixing wasted ad spend | 3 |

Each module unlocks after you complete the one before it. This ensures you're building on solid foundations.

> 📌 **Key Takeaway**: Modules must be completed in order. Module 1 unlocks after Module 0, Module 4 after Module 1, and so on. This is on purpose — each module builds on the previous one.

## Simulations — Where Theory Meets Practice

Simulations are the heart of AdCraft. This is where you **do the thing**, not just read about it.

| Simulation | What You'll Do | Prerequisite |
|-----------|----------------|--------------|
| **Campaign Builder** | Design complete campaign structures | Module 4 |
| **Bid Elevator** | Make 10 rapid bidding decisions with feedback | Module 6 |
| **STR Triage Arena** | Analyze 20 search terms and act on each one | Module 7 |

Each simulation has four phases:
1. **Briefing** — Get the mission context and product details
2. **Action** — Make your decisions (this is where the fun is)
3. **Scoring** — See your score with a breakdown
4. **Review** — Detailed feedback on each decision

> 💡 **Tip**: You can replay simulations as many times as you want. Your best score counts for XP, but the real value is in reviewing your mistakes and learning from them.

## AI Mentor — Your PPC Coach

The AI Mentor is like having a seasoned PPC expert on call 24/7. It knows:
- What module and lesson you're working on
- Your simulation scores and progress
- All the PPC rules and best practices from the curriculum

Use it when:
- You don't understand why a simulation scored you a certain way
- You want clarification on a metric or formula
- You need help deciding between two strategies
- You're curious about a PPC concept

> 🎯 **Analogy**: The AI Mentor is like a study buddy who's already taken the course and aced it. You ask questions, it gives you straight answers grounded in real PPC knowledge.

## XP and Leveling

You earn XP (experience points) by completing lessons and simulations:

| Action | XP You Earn |
|--------|------------|
| Complete a lesson | 50–100 XP |
| Complete a simulation | Up to 200 XP based on score |
| Perfect score bonus | +50 XP extra |
| Daily streak | +25 XP for logging in consecutive days |

You level up every 500 XP. Each level reflects your growing expertise:

| Level | Title | XP Needed |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Trainee | 0 |
| 2 | Analyst | 500 |
| 3 | Strategist | 1,000 |
| 4 | Commander | 1,500 |
| 5 | Expert | 2,000 |
