## Intent

Screen-led explainer for Filipino aspiring virtual assistants starting Amazon PPC training on Project Amazon PH Academy (AMPH). Teaches what the job actually is: a four-step loop (read, decide, change, explain), not button-pressing. Confident, calm, professional; no talking head beyond a possible open/close; the screen, brief, and artifacts carry the lesson. Source script: docs/VIDEO-EXPLAINER-SCRIPTS.md, script 1 "What an Amazon PPC VA Actually Does" (Module 0, before the platform tour).

## Notes

- Fully silent build: no narration audio, no music. Narration text from the source script is burned in as on-screen captions instead of TTS, since the script's own per-scene timestamps are already authoritative and more precise than anything a TTS-timing pass would produce.
- Production rules from docs/VIDEO-EXPLAINER-SCRIPTS.md apply: one decision per screen, high contrast, large numbers, no tiny screenshots, one clear closing action.
- This is the pilot of an 8-video pack; get this one right before batch-building the rest.
