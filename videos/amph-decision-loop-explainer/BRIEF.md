## Intent

Screen-led explainer teaching the PPC Decision Loop: a metric like ACoS at 40% means nothing on its own. Before acting, ask four ordered questions (objective, data maturity, traffic quality, conversion readiness), then walk through three worked examples (traffic/click-appeal problem, conversion problem, bidding problem) that show the same-looking data leading to different diagnoses and different fixes. Confident, calm, professional; no talking head; the screen, worked examples, and worksheet carry the lesson. Source script: docs/VIDEO-EXPLAINER-SCRIPTS.md, script 2 "The PPC Decision Loop" (Module 1, before the Big Six lesson).

## Notes

- Fully silent build: no narration audio, no music. Narration text from the source script is burned in as on-screen captions instead of TTS, since the script's own per-scene timestamps are already authoritative and more precise than anything a TTS-timing pass would produce.
- Production rules from docs/VIDEO-EXPLAINER-SCRIPTS.md apply: one decision per screen, high contrast, large numbers, no tiny screenshots, one clear closing action.
- Part of an 8-video pack; the pilot (script 1, amph-va-role-explainer) already validated the pipeline. This is script 2 of that pack.
