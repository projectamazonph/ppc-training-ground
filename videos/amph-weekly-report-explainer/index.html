The Five-Line Format
Use these five headings, in order.
1ResultState what happened.
2CauseExplain the likely reason.
3ActionState the change you made.
4WatchSay what you'll check next.
5RequestAsk for anything you need.
A Worked Example
One report, five honest lines.
Result
Ad sales rose 18% week over week, driven by our exact-match campaign.
Cause
ACoS also rose from 24% to 31% because CPC increased on two top-of-search terms.
Action
We reduced those bids to stay closer to the target and moved budget toward the better-converting targets.
Watch
We will review conversion rate and CPC next Tuesday.
Request
Please confirm whether the planned coupon will run next week.
