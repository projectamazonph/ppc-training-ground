## Intent

Screen-led explainer teaching the five-line weekly client report format: state the result, explain the likely cause, state the action taken, say what you'll watch next, and ask for anything needed from the client. Walks through a full worked example (18% week-over-week sales rise driven by the exact-match campaign; ACoS rising from 24% to 31% on two top-of-search terms; bids reduced and budget reallocated; conversion rate and CPC to be reviewed next Tuesday; a request to confirm an upcoming coupon), then highlights why plain language and named evidence build trust, before sending learners to complete the weekly report template. Confident, calm, professional; no talking head; the dense-dashboard-to-five-lines transformation, the five report headings, the worked example, and the template carry the lesson. Source script: docs/VIDEO-EXPLAINER-SCRIPTS.md, script 8 "Turn PPC Data Into a Weekly Client Update".
