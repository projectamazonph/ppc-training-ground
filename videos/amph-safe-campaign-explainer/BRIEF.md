## Intent

Screen-led explainer walking through the discipline of building a safe Sponsored Products campaign: start from the business brief (product, objective, budget, target ACoS, readiness), name campaigns so anyone can read them, separate structures by purpose (discovery, exact, product targeting, branded), set bids and budgets from max-CPC logic rather than console suggestions, run a launch QA checklist and capture a baseline, then open Campaign Builder to build from the keyword map. Confident, calm, professional; no talking head; the brief, naming formula, structure diagram, checklist, and Campaign Builder scenario entry carry the lesson. Source script: docs/VIDEO-EXPLAINER-SCRIPTS.md, script 6 "Build a Safe Sponsored Products Campaign" (Module 4, Campaign Architecture, directly before 4.4 Campaign Architecture Practice / Campaign Builder).

## Notes

- Fully silent build: no narration audio, no music. Narration text from the source script is burned in as on-screen captions instead of TTS, since the script's own per-scene timestamps are already authoritative and more precise than anything a TTS-timing pass would produce.
- Production rules from docs/VIDEO-EXPLAINER-SCRIPTS.md apply: one decision per screen, high contrast, large numbers, no tiny screenshots, one clear closing action.
